Privacy Policy for UniPic

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

I'm In It, LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the UniPic mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Camera Data

UniPic accesses your device’s camera to enable features such as image capture, identity verification, or other user‑initiated functionality. Camera access occurs only with your permission and only while using features that require it.

Location Data

UniPic may collect precise or approximate location information to enable location‑based features, improve service accuracy, or prevent fraud. Location data is collected only with your explicit consent and can be disabled through your device settings.

Biometric Information (Facial Recognition)

With your explicit consent, UniPic processes facial images and/or facial geometry data solely for identity verification or authentication purposes. Facial recognition data is considered biometric information and is treated as sensitive personal data.

We do not use biometric data for advertising purposes and do not sell biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate App features
  • Authenticate users
  • Enable security and fraud‑prevention measures
  • Improve App functionality
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Biometric Data Retention and Deletion

Facial recognition data is not retained beyond completion of the verification or authentication session, unless otherwise required to enable App functionality or comply with applicable law.

Users may withdraw consent at any time through device settings. Upon withdrawal of consent, biometric processing will stop, and any stored facial data (if applicable) will be deleted in accordance with applicable law.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal or biometric information. We may share information with trusted service providers who perform services on our behalf, subject to contractual confidentiality and security obligations. We may also disclose information if required by law.

6. Data Security

I'm In It, LLC implements reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of personal or biometric data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Limit or object to certain uses of your information

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.
